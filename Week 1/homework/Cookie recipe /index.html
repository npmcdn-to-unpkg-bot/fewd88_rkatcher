N0t Y0ur Grandm@'s Ch0c0late Chip C00kies

Prep Time: 45 Min

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon habanero pepper, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1¼ cup flour
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda

How to Make These Spicy Cookies:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Soften the butter, then in a large mixing bowl combine the butter and oil.
  3. Add in the habanero, brown sugar, regular sugar, egg, and vanilla. Mix until fully combined.
  4. Add the chocolate chips and continue to mix thoroughly.
  5. Spoon out the cookie dough onto an oiled cookie sheet, then bake the cookies for approximately 10 minutes or until the cookies are a golden brown.
  6. Let cookies rest for 3 to 5 minutes after baking on a rack, then serve.
